> **Standard**: This document follows [_wireframe_standard.md](./_wireframe_standard.md)

---

## 1. Overview

The Cart and Checkout flow is the final conversion point. The goal is to minimize friction, provide clear information, and ensure users complete their purchase confidently.

### Conversion Funnel
```
Cart â†’ Checkout (Shipping) â†’ Payment Selection â†’ Order Confirmation
```

---

## 2. User Stories

- As a **customer**, I want to review my cart items before checkout
- As a **buyer**, I want to update quantities or remove items easily
- As a **returning customer**, I want my shipping info pre-filled
- As a **mobile user**, I want a simple, linear checkout process
